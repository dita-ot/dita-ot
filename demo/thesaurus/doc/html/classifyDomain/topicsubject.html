topicsubject

Identifies the subject matter of content topics.

Description

You use the topicsubject element as follows:

  • Identify the primary subject of a content topic where one subject is more central to the topic's subject matter than any other.
  • Contain a list of secondary subjects that, in combination, identify the subject matter of the topic.

You nest the topicsubject element within the topicref element for the topic it classifies. You can embed topicsubject elements within a navigation hierarchy. See also http://www.w3.org/TR/swbp-skos-core-guide/#secindexing.
