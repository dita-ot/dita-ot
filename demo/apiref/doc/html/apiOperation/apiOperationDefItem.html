apiOperationDefItem

The <apiOperationDefItem> element is the specialization of the <apiDefItem> element to support classifier references and to distinguish returns, parameters, and events.

Description

In the <apiOperationDefItem> element, the <state>, <keyword>, and <ph> elements are provided to support unexpected situations within the general representation and to provide a basis for specialization. The <apiOperationDefItem> element is for nested substructure within returns, parameters, and events.
